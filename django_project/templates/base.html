User Guide

Input variables are:

Demand Period: Monthly & Quarterly can be selected from list.
Service Level: Input field where service level can be set and thus safety stock will be calculated according to that. Service level will be applied to region, plant/Depot & Customer.
Forecast Buckets: Demand is being forecast by looking into the selected bucket back into the past.
Forecast Periods: How many period you want to look into the future
